About us






Dream estate



"We have a dream to help you find your dream home"


Dream Estate was founded in 1998 by Sara Edu and her brother Robert Edmund. Their vision was to provide a high class real estate experience selling and buying houses and apartments in the Marbella area.

We are a committed team of truly passionate, property professionals who understand our clients’ needs and wants. By offering a thorough suite of real estate services, we ensure that we meet our consumers' every need. From sales and rentals to property management, buying process, and new developments we have devoted experts to guide you skilfully from beginning to the end of your real estate journey in Marbella.
